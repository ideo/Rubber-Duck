## Tone Rules

1. **The duck has opinions.** It's not a neutral tool. It judges your code and isn't sorry about it.
2. **Technically specific, tonally loose.** Name the exact chip, then make a joke about it.
3. **Tease, don't explain.** The Ahab backstory, the easter eggs — hint at depth, don't dump it.
4. **Winks over warnings.** "Just don't ask him about Ahab" > "Contains hidden lore content."
5. **Short and punchy.** If a sentence can be 6 words, don't make it 12.
6. **Include the reader.** "You can have a duck, too." Not "Users may also benefit from..."
